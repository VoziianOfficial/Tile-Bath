1. Overview

This website is an independent matching platform for homeowners seeking to compare local bathroom remodeling service providers. Cookies do not make Tile & Bath a contractor, remodeling installer, repair company, or provider of direct bathroom remodeling services.

Your browser controls matter

You can usually manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work as intended if certain cookies or storage technologies are blocked.

2. What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, SDKs, analytics identifiers, and scripts that help websites function or collect usage information.

These technologies may recognize your browser, remember preferences, support forms, measure page activity, or help understand how users interact with the site.

3. Types of cookies we may use

Essential cookies

Essential cookies and storage technologies help the website function. For example, they may remember whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice.

  • Cookie consent preference storage.
  • Basic site functionality and navigation support.
  • Security, spam prevention, or misuse prevention tools.

Analytics cookies

Analytics tools may help us understand general website performance, popular pages, form interaction, navigation patterns, and technical issues.

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages.
  • General device, browser, and session information.
  • Interactions with buttons, forms, and navigation links.

Advertising and conversion cookies

Advertising or conversion technologies may help measure whether marketing campaigns, ads, or referral sources lead to quote request activity.

  • Campaign performance measurement.
  • Conversion tracking for quote requests or contact actions.
  • General attribution signals, where applicable.

Preference cookies

Preference technologies may remember choices you make on the site, such as privacy notice interactions or basic interface preferences.

4. Third-party tools and providers

We may use third-party tools for analytics, advertising measurement, form routing, security, hosting, performance, or provider matching support. These third parties may set or read cookies and similar technologies according to their own policies.

If you submit a quote request, your information may also be shared with independent providers, contractor networks, or service partners as described in our Privacy Policy.

Tile & Bath is not responsible for the cookie or privacy practices of third-party websites, independent providers, contractor networks, advertising platforms, or external services.

5. Cookie consent choices

When you first visit the site, you may see a cookie notice with options to accept or decline. Your choice may be stored in your browser using local storage or a similar technology so the banner does not need to appear on every page view.

If you decline cookies, some non-essential tracking or preference functions may be limited where technically supported. Essential technologies may still be used when necessary for website operation, security, or compliance.

Clearing browser data may remove your stored preference and cause the cookie notice to appear again.

6. How to manage cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies. The exact steps depend on the browser and device you use.

  • Block some or all cookies.
  • Delete cookies already stored on your device.
  • Use private or incognito browsing modes.
  • Adjust tracking prevention or privacy settings.
  • Use browser extensions or device-level privacy tools.

Blocking cookies may affect website functionality, form behavior, analytics accuracy, saved preferences, and certain conversion measurement features.

7. Do Not Track signals

Some browsers may send “Do Not Track” signals. Because there is not a single consistent industry standard for responding to these signals, this website may not respond to all Do Not Track browser settings in a uniform way.

Quote request information submitted by you may be processed separately from cookie data and may be shared with independent providers or service partners as described in our Privacy Policy.
