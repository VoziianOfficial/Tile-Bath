Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tile & Bath may collect, use, share, and protect information submitted through this website and related quote request forms.

Effective date: January 1, 2026 USA service area

Aggregator privacy notice

Tile & Bath is not a direct bathroom remodeling contractor. Information submitted through the site may be used to help connect homeowners with independent local providers.

Tile & Bath is an independent matching platform and does not perform bathroom remodeling, plumbing, tile installation, demolition, construction, design, or contractor services directly. Providers are independent third parties. Homeowners are responsible for verifying licensing, insurance, estimates, project scope, materials, timelines, warranties, and contract terms.

1. Overview

Tile & Bath operates as an independent matching platform that helps homeowners submit bathroom remodeling project requests and compare potential local provider options. The site does not provide bathroom remodeling, replacement, repair, tile installation, energy-efficiency upgrades, custom design, inspections, measurements, or contractor services directly.

By using this website or submitting a form, you acknowledge that your information may be processed as described in this Privacy Policy.

Important platform note

Independent providers may have their own privacy practices. You should review any provider’s privacy policy or notices directly before sharing additional information with them.

2. Information we may collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through forms, calls, emails, buttons, or other website interactions.

Information you provide

  • Name and contact details, such as phone number and email address.
  • Project ZIP code, city, state, or general service location.
  • Bathroom remodeling project category, such as installation, replacement, repair, glass replacement, vanity & sink upgrades, or accessible bathroom updates.
  • Project timing, notes, preferences, and details you choose to submit.
  • Consent confirmations and form submission records.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information.
  • IP address and approximate location signals.
  • Pages visited, links clicked, session activity, and general website usage data.
  • Cookie and similar technology information, where applicable.

3. How we may use information

We may use collected information to operate the website, respond to requests, support provider matching, improve user experience, and maintain legal and operational records.

  • To process and route quote requests.
  • To help identify independent local providers that may be relevant to the project category and service area.
  • To communicate with you about your request.
  • To maintain website functionality, security, and performance.
  • To analyze general site usage and improve page content, forms, and user experience.
  • To comply with applicable legal, compliance, or network requirements.
  • To prevent fraud, misuse, spam, or unauthorized activity.

4. Sharing information with independent providers

When you submit a project request, we may share relevant information with independent local providers, contractor networks, lead partners, or service partners so they may contact you about your bathroom remodeling-related request.

Shared information may include your name, contact details, project category, project location, timing, and submitted notes. These providers are independent from Tile & Light and are responsible for their own communications, estimates, appointments, pricing, services, warranties, and privacy practices.

Tile & Bath does not guarantee that a provider will contact you, offer a quote, serve your area, or accept your project.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

This website may use cookies, local storage, pixels, analytics tools, and similar technologies to support site functionality and understand general website usage.

  • Essential cookies may help the site remember basic preferences, such as cookie consent choices.
  • Analytics cookies may help understand how visitors interact with pages and forms.
  • Advertising or conversion technologies may help measure quote request activity or campaign performance.

You can review more details on our Cookie Policy page.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain privacy rights regarding your personal information. These may include the ability to request access, correction, deletion, or information about how personal data is used or shared.

You may also choose not to submit a quote request form. However, some website features may require contact or project information in order to process a request.

To make a privacy-related request, contact us using the email listed on this page.

7. Data security and retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information submitted through the website. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to process requests, support business operations, meet compliance obligations, resolve disputes, prevent misuse, or satisfy legal requirements.

8. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking information about bathroom remodeling provider options. It is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Third-party websites and providers

This website may contain links to third-party websites or may help connect users with independent providers. Tile & Bath is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or actions of third parties.

You should review third-party privacy policies directly before submitting additional information to any provider, contractor, advertiser, or external website.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you acknowledge the updated policy.
