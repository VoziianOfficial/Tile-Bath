Company story

A focused platform for one of the most visible parts of a home.

Bathroom remodeling affects comfort, privacy, storage, durability, and the daily feel of a room. Yet the first step of finding provider options can feel scattered: different categories, different terminology, different quote processes, and different levels of detail.

Tile & Bath was created to make that first step feel more organized. Instead of positioning itself as a contractor, the platform helps homeowners choose a bathroom service category, share project basics, and request contact from independent local providers.

The goal is practical clarity: homeowners should be able to compare provider responses, ask better questions, and move forward only after reviewing details directly with the companies they are considering.

Aggregator model

What Tile & Bath does — and what it does not do.

Organizes the request

The platform helps homeowners identify the right bathroom remodeling service and submit basic project details in a structured way.

Supports provider matching

Requests may be routed toward independent provider options that appear relevant to the service category and location.

Encourages comparison

Homeowners are encouraged to compare estimates, timelines, warranty terms, materials, licensing, insurance, and communication style.

Important

Does not perform work directly

Tile & Bath is an independent matching platform and does not perform bathroom remodeling, plumbing, tile installation, demolition, construction, design, or contractor services directly. All work discussions happen directly between homeowners and independent providers.

Homeowner responsibility

The final decision should be based on verified provider details.

Tile & Bath keeps the platform language clear because remodeling work should not be confused with matching support. Before hiring any company, homeowners should review project-specific information directly with that provider.
